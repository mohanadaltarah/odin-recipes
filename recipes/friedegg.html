The recipe of "Fried eggs"

Mohanad's friedegg

Fast, delicious, and healthy. This is how I describe this dish

it is the best for the breakfast and as a post-workout meal.

The ingredients:

  • 1 onion
  • 5 full eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp black piper
  • 1 tsp olive oil

The steps:

First slice the onion into thin slices. when the pan gets heated, put the oil

then let the oil heats and put the thin slices of onion inside. let them be cooked and break the eggs above them

put all the spices, put heat on the lowest, and cover the pan. Wait for 10 minutes and your egg pan will be ready to be eaten

you can eat cucumber with it, I do that.
