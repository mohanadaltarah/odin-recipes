Mohanad's Apple Oatmeal

This is one of many different kinds of recipes Mohanad makes every morning.

Ingredients are:

  • 50g of Oats
  • 3 pieces of date
  • 1 apple sliced into small cubes
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon or cacao
  • 1/2 cup of any kind of milk
  • 1 cube of no sugar chocolate
  • 15g of nuts
  • 1 tsp of tahini
  • 1 tsp of Grape molasses

The steps::

You blind everything except chocalate, tahini, nut, grape molasses, and a few apple cubes, Then you put the mixture in the microwave from about 3 min depending on yours and lastly you put the chocolate to melt down, the nuts over it, tahini, and grape molasses and you are good to go. Have a nice meal
