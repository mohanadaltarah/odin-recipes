The recipe of "Mujaddara"

The Syrian Mujaddara

one of the most famous cuisines of the syria kitchen

it consists of healthy ingredients and it is considered vegan if you are one :)

The ingredients:

  • 1 cup of lentils
  • 1 cup of cracked wheat
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp black piper
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 5 cups water

The steps:

First you put the pot on the stove. when the pot gets heated, put the oil

then let the oil heats and put the thin slices of onion inside. let them be cooked and prepare the lentils

put the lentils with 5 cups of water. cover the pot and lower the heat. wait for 10 minutes and put the cracked wheat with all spices we mentioned and stir to mix everything

wait for 20 minutes and your dish will be ready. Enjoy.
